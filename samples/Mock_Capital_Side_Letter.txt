SIDE LETTER AGREEMENT

This Side Letter Agreement (this "Side Letter") is entered into as of February 1, 2024, 
by and between Mock Fund I, LP (the "Partnership") and Mock Capital LP (the "Limited Partner").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Partnership and the Limited Partner have entered into that certain Limited 
Partnership Agreement dated as of January 15, 2024 (the "Partnership Agreement"); and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to modify certain terms of the Partnership Agreement as they 
apply to the Limited Partner.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties 
agree as follows:

ARTICLE 3 - MANAGEMENT FEES

Section 3.2 - Management Fee Reduction
Notwithstanding Section 6.1 of the Partnership Agreement, the Management Fee payable by 
the Limited Partner shall be reduced to 1.75% per annum of the Limited Partner's Capital 
Commitment during the Investment Period.

Section 3.3 - Fee Step-Down
Beginning on the fourth anniversary of the Initial Closing, the Management Fee shall be 
further reduced by 0.25% per annum for the remainder of the Partnership term.

ARTICLE 5 - MOST FAVORED NATION

Section 5.1 - MFN Protection
If the Partnership enters into a side letter with any other Limited Partner that contains 
terms more favorable than those set forth herein, the Partnership shall promptly notify 
the Limited Partner and, upon written request, shall extend such more favorable terms to 
the Limited Partner.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Side Letter as of the date first 
written above.
