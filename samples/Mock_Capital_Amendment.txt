FIRST AMENDMENT TO SIDE LETTER AGREEMENT

This First Amendment (this "Amendment") is entered into as of June 15, 2024, by and 
between Mock Fund I, LP (the "Partnership") and Mock Capital LP (the "Limited Partner").

RECITALS

WHEREAS, the Partnership and the Limited Partner entered into that certain Side Letter 
Agreement dated as of February 1, 2024 (the "Original Side Letter"); and

WHEREAS, the parties wish to amend certain terms of the Original Side Letter.

NOW, THEREFORE, in consideration of the mutual covenants contained herein, the parties 
agree as follows:

1. AMENDMENT TO SECTION 3.2

Section 3.2 of the Original Side Letter is hereby amended and restated in its entirety 
to read as follows:

"Section 3.2 - Management Fee Reduction
Notwithstanding Section 6.1 of the Partnership Agreement, the Management Fee payable 
by the Limited Partner shall be reduced to 1.50% per annum of the Limited Partner's 
Capital Commitment during the Investment Period."

2. NEW SECTION 3.4 - FEE WAIVER

The following new Section 3.4 is hereby added to the Original Side Letter:

"Section 3.4 - Fee Waiver
For the calendar year 2024, the Partnership shall waive 25% of the Management Fee 
otherwise payable by the Limited Partner, in recognition of the Limited Partner's 
early commitment to the Partnership."

3. CONTINUATION OF ORIGINAL SIDE LETTER

Except as expressly modified by this Amendment, all terms and conditions of the 
Original Side Letter shall remain in full force and effect.

4. EFFECTIVE DATE

This Amendment shall be effective as of June 15, 2024.

IN WITNESS WHEREOF, the parties have executed this Amendment as of the date first 
written above.
