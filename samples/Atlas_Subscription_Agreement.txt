MOCK FUND II, LP

SUBSCRIPTION AGREEMENT

Date: January 20, 2024
Investor: Atlas Family Office

1. SUBSCRIPTION

The undersigned (the "Investor") hereby subscribes for a limited partnership interest 
in Mock Fund II, LP (the "Partnership") and agrees to make a Capital Commitment of 
FIFTY MILLION DOLLARS ($50,000,000 USD).

2. CAPITAL CONTRIBUTIONS

2.1 The Investor agrees to contribute capital to the Partnership in accordance with 
capital calls issued by the General Partner from time to time.

2.2 Capital calls shall be made with not less than ten (10) business days' prior 
written notice.

3. MANAGEMENT FEE

3.1 During the Investment Period, the Investor shall pay a Management Fee equal to 
2.00% per annum of the Investor's Capital Commitment.

3.2 Following the Investment Period, the Management Fee shall be calculated based on 
the Investor's share of invested capital.

4. CARRIED INTEREST

4.1 The General Partner shall be entitled to a Carried Interest equal to 20% of the 
net profits of the Partnership, subject to a Preferred Return of 8% per annum.

5. CO-INVESTMENT RIGHTS

5.1 The Investor shall have the right, but not the obligation, to co-invest alongside 
the Partnership in any portfolio investment, on a pro-rata basis.

6. REPRESENTATIONS AND WARRANTIES

The Investor represents and warrants that:

(a) The Investor is an "accredited investor" as defined in Regulation D;
(b) The Investor has sufficient knowledge and experience in financial matters;
(c) The Investor can bear the economic risk of the investment.
