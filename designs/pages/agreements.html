SIDE LETTER AGREEMENT

This Side Letter Agreement (this "Side Letter") is entered into as of February 1, 2024, by and between Mock Fund I, LP (the "Partnership") and Mock Capital LP (the "Limited Partner").

3.2 Management Fee. Notwithstanding Section 6.1 of the Partnership Agreement, the Management Fee payable by the Limited Partner shall be reduced to 1.75% per annum of the Limited Partner's Capital Commitment during the Investment Period.

3.3 Fee Step-Down. Beginning on the fourth anniversary of the Initial Closing, the Management Fee shall be further reduced by 0.25% per annum for the remainder of the Partnership term.
